About the Copa America 2024

La Copa América 2024 will be the 48th edition of this tournament, the main football competition among the national teams of South America and the oldest in the world. It will be co-organized by Conmebol and Concacaf. For the second time, the tournament will take place in the United States since the last time during the centennial edition in 2016. It will be contested from June 20 to July 14, 2024.
